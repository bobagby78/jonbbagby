What's Sup?

The Dungeon

What's Sup was a group capstone project I completed with three other people during the optional Liftoff extension to the LaunchCode Web development training I attended in 2020. The main features of the app were the "My Kitchen" view, where users could keep track of the proteins, spices, condiments and sides in their pantry via form submission. Users can also upload recipes to the database to choose from on the "Recipes" view. Once the database is loaded with pantry items and recipes, users can then use the "Match Recipes" feature which shows a list of recipes the user can cook based on their pantry items and the recipes in the database. Users would be able to choose from their own recipes to match, or other user's recipes.

My Garage is my next project on the horizon. It will be a java-based web app (and will most likely have a Kotlin Android counterpart) designed for the home mechanic. It will have a garage portal where users can import their vehicles that they own and work on to save to the database. It will have a service portal where users can keep track of regular maintenance performed on each of the vehicles in their garage. I would also like to play around with a social aspect where users can reach out to others on the app and ask questions, share photos, etc.
